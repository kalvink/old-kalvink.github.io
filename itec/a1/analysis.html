Market Analysis

According to the Market Analysis Survey located below, this new information
has influenced the design of the website drastically.The website is made to
reflect a restaurant as Global Dining serves food to customers of any age,
gender, income, and education. I chose a standard neutral design and page color
as they aren't specifically targeting any type of demographic. I made sure to
include plenty of photos of their best selling take out dishes for customers to
order. I made sure to make the photos big to allow the customers to capture the
quality just by looking at it. I've chosen family friendly images for the homepage
because this is a restaurant for all ages.
